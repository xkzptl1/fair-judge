# Product Overview  
  
## What this product is  
  
This is a multi-perspective news exploration app.  
  
It helps users understand how the same topic is framed differently across media sources.  
  
This is NOT a traditional news reader.  
  
---  
  
## Core Idea  
  
Instead of showing articles in a feed, this product:  
  
- groups articles by topic  
- classifies how each article frames the topic  
- visualizes disagreement and perspective differences  
- highlights ambiguity instead of hiding it  
  
---  
  
## User Value  
  
Users should be able to:  
  
- quickly understand what a topic is about  
- see how opinions differ across sources  
- identify whether coverage is one-sided or balanced  
- explore different viewpoints easily  
  
---  
  
## Key Differentiator  
  
Most news apps optimize for:  
  
- engagement  
- speed  
- volume  
  
This app optimizes for:  
  
- clarity  
- structure  
- understanding  
  
---  
  
## Core UX Philosophy  
  
Before reading articles, users should understand:  
  
- what the main issue is  
- how perspectives are distributed  
- where disagreements exist  
  
---  
  
## Anti-Goals  
  
- Do not become a news feed  
- Do not optimize for clicks or scrolling  
- Do not force binary interpretations  
- Do not hide ambiguity}
